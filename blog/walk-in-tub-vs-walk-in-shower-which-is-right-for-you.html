Walk-In Tub vs Walk-In Shower: Which Is Right for You?

Choosing between a walk-in tub and a walk-in shower is a big decision, especially when planning for aging in place or improved safety.

Walk-In Showers

Best for: Active adults who want easy access, modern aesthetics, and low maintenance. Our All-Star Shower Systems are completely grout-free and can be customized with seating, grab bars, and multiple shower heads.

Aging Safely Walk-In Tubs

Best for: Seniors or those with mobility challenges who want a safe bathing option with a door and built-in seating. Many models include therapeutic jets and quick-drain technology.

Our Recommendation

Many Conway area homeowners choose a combination approach — a walk-in shower for daily use and a walk-in tub for therapeutic bathing. We can help you design the perfect solution for your needs and budget.
