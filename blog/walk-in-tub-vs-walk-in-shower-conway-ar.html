Walk-In Tub vs Walk-In Shower: Which is Right for You?

Conway & Central Arkansas • July 2026

Choosing between a walk-in tub and a walk-in shower is one of the biggest decisions for homeowners in Conway, Cabot, and Central Arkansas who want to age in place safely.

Quick Comparison

Walk-In Shower (All-Star Shower System)
Best for: Most people who can stand safely with support. Faster installation, lower cost, easier to clean, more modern look.
Walk-In Tub
Best for: People who have difficulty standing or want to soak. Higher price, longer installation, but excellent for soaking and relaxation.

Our Recommendation

For most seniors and families in Central Arkansas, we recommend starting with an **All-Star Shower System** (especially Signature or Designer tier) because of the faster install, lower cost, and excellent safety features with zero threshold and grab bars.

However, if soaking is important for pain relief or mobility is very limited, a walk-in tub can be the better choice.
